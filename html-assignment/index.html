About Me.

I was born on March 28th 2002.

I was raised here in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. Growing up, I would go to Mexico to visit my extended family every year, whether that was for Christmas or a few weeks during the summer. But now that I'm older and more busy with school and other activities, I don't go as often as I'd like.

Before coming to the Interactive Media Design program at Algonquin, I was at the University of Ottawa studying in the Fine Arts program. I spent 4 years studying techniques, theories and history of art. In my final year of the program, I spent the last two terms (as well as other years) creating works related to Mexican culture, focusing on reconnection with my family roots and Mexican traditions that I dont get to experience from while living in Canada. The IMD program is quite a change and different experience from the BFA Visual arts program at the UOttawa, but I have been enjoying it so far.

This is a quote I really like:

If you cant fly, then run, if you can't run, then walk, if you can't walk then crawl, but whatever you do, you have to keep moving forward. -Martin Luther King Jr.

About Me.

I was born on March 28th 2002.

I was raised here in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. Growing up, I would go to Mexico to visit my extended family every year, whether that was for Christmas or a few weeks during the summer. But now that I'm older and more busy with school and other activities, I don't go as often as I'd like.


Before coming to the Interactive Media Design program at Algonquin, I was at the University of Ottawa studying in the Fine Arts program. I spent 4 years studying techniques, theories and history of art. In my final year of the program, I spent the last two terms (as well as other years) creating works related to Mexican culture, focusing on reconnection with my family roots and Mexican traditions that I dont get to experience from while living in Canada.
